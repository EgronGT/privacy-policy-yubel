The data controller is the publisher of the App on app stores (Google Play / App Store), identified above. For privacy questions, to exercise your rights, or for general support, contact us at supportyubel@gmail.com. You can also use any in-app contact options we provide.

2. Data We Collect

Depending on how you use the App, we may collect and process the following categories of data:

  • Account and profile data: name, email address, password (stored securely and never in plain text), age, gender, profile photos, bio, interests, and other information you choose to provide when registering or editing your profile.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates or approximate location (e.g. city or distance) when you enable location-based features. Location is used to show you nearby users and is not collected in the background.
  • Camera and microphone: accessed only when you actively use video calling or photo capture features. We do not access the camera or microphone in the background. These permissions can be revoked at any time in your device settings.
  • Photos and media: images you upload or capture through the App, stored on our servers to provide the service.
  • Messages and interactions: messages sent through the App's chat features, match interactions (likes, passes, unmatches), and similar activity data.
  • Technical and device data: device model, operating system version, app version, installation identifiers, push notification tokens, crash logs, and network connection data used to operate and improve the App.
  • In-app purchase data: subscription status and purchase verification data, managed by app stores and RevenueCat (see Section 4). We do not store full payment card information.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Providing the service (matching, profile display, in-app communications, video calls): necessary for the performance of our contract with you.
  • Location-based matching: to show you profiles of nearby users; requires your consent via device permission.
  • Push notifications (matches, messages, activity): with your consent, revocable at any time in device or in-app settings.
  • Subscription management: to verify active subscriptions and unlock premium features.
  • Safety, abuse prevention, and moderation: to detect and act on violations of our terms, based on legitimate interests and legal obligations.
  • App improvement: anonymized or aggregated usage analytics to understand how the App is used and improve features, based on legitimate interests.
  • Legal compliance: to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

No system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at supportyubel@gmail.com.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law (e.g. for tax, legal, or safety reasons) or to resolve disputes. Anonymized or aggregated data may be retained indefinitely for analytics purposes.

8. Account Deletion

How to delete your account and data:
You can permanently delete your Yubel account and all associated data directly from the app at any time.

Steps to delete your account in the app:

  1. Open the Yubel app and log in.
  2. Tap your profile icon to open your profile.
  3. Go to Settings.
  4. Scroll down to the Account section.
  5. Tap Delete account.
  6. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your profile data, photos, matches, and messages will be permanently removed. Some data may be retained for a short period as required by law or to resolve any outstanding disputes.

If you are unable to access the app, you may also request account deletion by emailing supportyubel@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days.

  • Rectification: request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten"): request deletion of your data (see also Section 8 for in-app account deletion).
  • Restriction: request that we limit the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, commonly used format, where technically feasible.
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent: where processing is based on your consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.
  • Complaint: lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (in Italy: Garante per la protezione dei dati personali).

To exercise any of these rights, contact supportyubel@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
