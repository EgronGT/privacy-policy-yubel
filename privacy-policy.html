Privacy Policy

App: Yubel
Last updated: 10 May 2026
Data controller: Independent publisher and operator of the Yubel mobile application
Privacy & support: supportyubel@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Yubel (the “App”) processes personal data, in line with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”) where it applies, and with other applicable data protection laws.

1. Data controller

The data controller is the publisher of the App on app stores (Google Play / App Store), identified above. To exercise the rights described in Section 8, for privacy questions, or for general support, contact supportyubel@gmail.com. You can also use any in-app contact options we provide, when available.

2. Categories of data we process

Depending on how you use the App, we may process the following types of data:

  • Account and profile data: for example name, email, password (handled securely on our backend), age, gender, profile fields, profile photos, interests, and other information you choose to provide.
  • Location data: coordinates or location-derived information (e.g. city) when you enable location-based features or enter a place.
  • Live audio/video: for video calling or similar real-time features, the App may access the camera and microphone with your permission. Media streams are processed as needed to provide the service (including real-time signaling). We do not use the camera or microphone in the background without your active use of that feature.
  • Photos and images: if you upload or capture images through the App, those files may be processed and stored as required for the service.
  • Technical and usage data: device or installation identifiers, technical logs, push notification tokens, and connection data needed to run the App.
  • In-app purchase data: information handled by app stores and our subscription provider (see Section 4) to verify subscription status.

3. Purposes and legal bases

  • Providing the service (dating features, profile, in-app communications): performance of a contract or pre-contractual steps; where required, your consent (e.g. sensitive device permissions).
  • Notifications (if enabled): consent or legitimate interests, according to system and in-app settings.
  • Security, abuse prevention, and legal compliance: legitimate interests and legal obligations.

4. Recipients and processors

Data may be shared with:

  • Our backend and infrastructure providers that host the App’s API, databases, and related services.
  • Object storage providers (e.g. cloud storage) used to store profile photos and similar media you upload.
  • Google (Firebase) for services such as push messaging (Firebase Cloud Messaging), under Google’s terms and privacy notices.
  • Google Maps for map display and related features, under applicable Google notices.
  • RevenueCat for technical management of subscriptions and in-app purchases, limited to what is necessary for that purpose.
  • Google Play / Apple App Store for distribution and payments, under their terms.

You may request further detail on subprocessors by emailing supportyubel@gmail.com.

5. International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards under GDPR (such as standard contractual clauses), where applicable.

6. Retention

We keep data for as long as needed for the purposes for which it was collected (e.g. for the lifetime of your account and related services), unless a longer retention period is required by law or for legal claims. After account deletion, data may be erased or anonymized according to our technical timelines and policies, except where the law requires retention.

7. Whether provision is mandatory

Providing data needed for registration and core features is necessary to use those features. If you do not provide it, we may not be able to provide the service. Device permissions (camera, microphone, location, notifications) are optional and can be revoked in system settings, but some features may not work without them.

8. Your rights

Where GDPR applies, you may have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability (for data you provided, where applicable), and object, subject to legal limits. You may withdraw consent where processing is based on consent, without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal. You may lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority in your country, in particular in the EEA. To exercise these rights or ask questions, contact supportyubel@gmail.com.

9. Children

The App is not directed at children under the minimum age required by applicable law (often 16 in the EEA, or 13 in the US with applicable parental consent rules). We do not knowingly collect data from children below that threshold. If you believe a child has provided data, contact supportyubel@gmail.com to request deletion.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The date at the top shows the latest revision. For material changes, we may provide notice through the App or other appropriate channels.
