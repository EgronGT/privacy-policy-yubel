Child Safety Standards

App: Yubel  |  Last updated: May 12, 2026

1. Age Requirement

Yubel is a dating application intended exclusively for users aged 18 and older. Users are required to confirm their age during registration. Accounts found to belong to minors are immediately suspended and permanently deleted.

2. Zero Tolerance for Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)

Yubel has a strict zero-tolerance policy toward any content that sexually exploits or endangers minors. Any user found sharing, distributing, or requesting such content will be permanently banned, and the incident will be reported to the relevant national authorities and the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) where required by law.

3. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report any content or behavior that they believe involves the exploitation or endangerment of a minor directly within the app or by contacting us at:

Email: yubelsupport@gmail.com

All reports are reviewed promptly. Accounts subject to a report are suspended pending investigation.

4. Compliance

Yubel complies with all applicable local and international laws regarding child safety, including but not limited to laws on the prevention of child sexual exploitation and abuse. We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies when required.

5. Contact

For any questions or concerns regarding child safety on Yubel, please contact us at:
